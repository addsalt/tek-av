We are an Ottawa-based contracting service specializing in the installation of home comfort, entertainment and technology. Whether you are fitting your new home, retrofitting your old home, or setting up your new business, Tek-AV can help.

Achieve your
dream setup.

Every client’s needs are different and we want you to access our services on your terms. We provide client tailored solutions, performing installation, troubleshooting, tutorials and remote programming for both residential and commercial clients in audio, video and networking.

Custom-crafted
expertise.

As local business-owners, we believe if we succeed, a part of the community succeeds. We work with trusted licensed electricians and trades people, and will make any referrals required to complete your vision right and on time.
